HVAC Systems

We provide comprehensive HVAC solutions including VRF systems, chilled water systems, conventional split/package units, PHE systems, spot cooling systems, and industrial ventilation systems tailored to your specific requirements.

VRF systems offer superior energy efficiency (30-40% savings), zonal temperature control, quiet operation, flexible installation, simultaneous heating/cooling capability, and reduced maintenance costs compared to traditional systems.

Yes, we offer comprehensive AMC services including preventive maintenance, emergency repairs, system optimization, filter cleaning, refrigerant charging, and performance monitoring to ensure optimal HVAC operation.

We use advanced load calculation software, select energy-efficient equipment, implement proper insulation, design optimized ductwork, incorporate heat recovery systems, and provide building automation for maximum energy savings.

With 30+ years of experience, we have successfully executed industrial HVAC projects for manufacturing plants, warehouses, pharmaceutical units, food processing facilities, and clean rooms with specialized requirements.

Electrical & Plumbing Systems

We offer complete electrical solutions including power cabling, terminations, electrical panel supply & installations, lighting systems, power distribution, emergency power systems, and building automation integration.

Yes, we specialize in high-voltage electrical systems for industrial and commercial projects, including transformer installations, HT/LT panels, substation works, and power distribution up to 33kV capacity.

We install comprehensive plumbing systems including water supply networks, drainage systems, STP (Sewage Treatment Plant) systems, ETP (Effluent Treatment Plant) systems, sanitary works, and industrial process plumbing.

We design water-efficient systems using low-flow fixtures, rainwater harvesting, greywater recycling, optimized pipe sizing, pressure reducing valves, and water-efficient STP/ETP systems to minimize water consumption.

Yes, we provide complete electrical safety compliance certificates, test reports, and statutory approvals as per IE rules, NBC guidelines, and local authority requirements for all our electrical installations.

Fire Safety & Project Management

We install comprehensive fire safety systems including fire hydrants, sprinkler systems, fire alarm systems (addressable & conventional), fire suppression systems, emergency lighting, and fire detection systems.

Addressable systems pinpoint exact device locations during alarms, provide detailed system information, and allow individual device monitoring. Conventional systems indicate zone-level alarms only. Addressable systems offer superior performance for larger buildings.

We use advanced project management tools, create detailed schedules with critical path method, conduct regular progress reviews, coordinate with multiple trades, and employ efficient resource allocation to ensure timely project completion.

Yes, we provide comprehensive BIM services including 3D modeling, clash detection, coordination drawings, shop drawings, and as-built documentation to ensure seamless integration of all MEP systems and avoid on-site conflicts.

We implement rigorous quality control through material inspections, workmanship checks, system testing, compliance verification, documentation review, and third-party audits to ensure all MEP installations meet the highest quality standards.
