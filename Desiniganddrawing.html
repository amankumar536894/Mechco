System Design & Drawing

At MECHCO PROJECTS, hum System Design & Drawing ko ek strategic service ke roop me dekhte hain. Har project ke liye hum detailed engineering layouts, schematic diagrams, 3D models aur preparation of construction drawings provide karte hain, taaki site teams ko execution mein clarity aur accuracy mile.

🔧 Humare Key Design Services

Service Details
Mechanical & HVAC Design Complete HVAC layouts, ducting design, VRF / chilled water systems, ventilation & air distribution drawings
Piping & Process Design Piping isometrics, P&ID drawings, stress analysis, routing & supports
Structural & Civil Integration Integration of structural elements with services: supports, penetrations, coordination
Electrical & Power Distribution Single line diagrams, load flow, panel layouts, cable routing, earthing
Instrumentation & Controls Control system schematics, sensor & actuator layouts, instrumentation piping
3D Modeling & BIM Drawings 3D models for clash detection, coordination, with detailed drawings generated from model
Construction / Fabrication Drawings Field installation drawings, shop drawings, fabrication details
BOQ & Material Schedules Bill of Quantities, material lists, tag schedules, cut-lists etc.
