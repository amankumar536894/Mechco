MEP FAQ's

MEP Engineering Questions

MECHCO provides complete Mechanical, Electrical, Plumbing & Fire Safety solutions including HVAC systems (VRF & Chilled Water), electrical installations, STP/ETP systems, fire protection systems, and comprehensive MEP project management from design to commissioning.

VRF (Variable Refrigerant Flow) systems offer superior energy efficiency, zonal temperature control, quiet operation, and flexible installation. They can reduce energy consumption by 30-40% compared to conventional systems, making them ideal for commercial and large residential projects.

Yes, we ensure 100% statutory compliance for all MEP installations. Our team handles all necessary approvals, certifications, and compliance with IS codes, NBC guidelines, fire safety norms, electrical regulations, and environmental standards for STP/ETP systems.

With 3+ years of experience, we have executed 400+ MEP projects across 15 Lakhs sq.ft, including industrial facilities, manufacturing plants, warehouses, and processing units. We specialize in industrial cooling, power distribution, process plumbing, and industrial fire safety systems.

Yes, we offer comprehensive AMC (Annual Maintenance Contracts) for all MEP systems including preventive maintenance, emergency repairs, system optimization, and performance monitoring to ensure optimal operation and extended equipment life.

MEP project timelines vary based on scale: Residential projects (3-6 months), Commercial buildings (6-12 months), Industrial facilities (8-18 months). We provide detailed project schedules with milestones and ensure timely completion through efficient project management.

Yes, we utilize advanced BIM (Building Information Modeling) technology for MEP coordination, clash detection, and optimized system design. This ensures seamless integration of all MEP services, reduces on-site conflicts, and enhances project efficiency.

We provide energy-efficient MEP solutions including VRF systems, LED lighting with smart controls, solar-ready electrical systems, water-efficient plumbing fixtures, heat recovery systems, and building automation for optimal energy management and cost savings.
